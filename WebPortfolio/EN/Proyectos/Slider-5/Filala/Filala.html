Filalá

In collaboration with MARA JIMENEZ,
NATALIA SPEZZA and LUCÍA B. ROVASIO AGUIRRE

Filalá is an application project for purchasing tickets for musical events. Our research showed that the stress related to purchasing tickets is due to a lack of trust in current ticket platforms: the virtual queues tend to crash due to the high demand, and the very short time for inserting sensitive information can lead to type errors.

This app seeks to reduce the stress related to the virtual queue at massive events by using the waiting time to load purchase and sensible data more calmly.
