Escuadrón Atrapasueños

In collaboration with NATALIA SPEZZA and
IAN COSANO CATTANEO

Escuadrón Atrapasueños is a project of a toys-to-life videogame. We designed the plot of the story, as well as the characters and villains.

The plot takes place in the Realm of Dreams, which has been invaded by Umbra and its dark nightmares. To save the kingdom, King Aelios sends the Escuadrón Atrapasueños to the human world, hoping that some human child will help defeat Umbra and the shadows.
