ASTROLANDIA

In collaboration with MARA JIMENEZ and IAN COSANO CATTANEO

Astrolandia is an interactive and immersive experience where the basic astrology concepts of the zodiac signs are explained.

The experience is divided if four instances, each representing represents one of the four elements (fire, earth, air and water) and explaining the zodiac signs associated with it. Every elemental-world is represented with a 2D and 3D animation, and an original song composed for that element.

The instances are started by the visitor, who presses the elemental button of the controller, placed in the center of the room.
