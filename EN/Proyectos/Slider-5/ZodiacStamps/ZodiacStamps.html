Zodiac Stamps

This project was a personal exploration through the technique of digital collage. The idea was to create a system of mini-collages of my interpretation of the twelve zodiac signs.

Thanks to my knowledge of astrology and its symbolism, I created these collage with the according color palettes and shapes that represent each signs energy.
