CONTRATIEMPOS

In collaboration with MARA JIMENEZ and IAN COSANO CATANEO

Contratiempos is a Virtual Reality project, where the visitor experiences the trip from a fantasy dream to a creepy nightmare.

With the help of 3D modelling and sound design, we created these low poly sceneries for an experience that plays with our dreams, where we cannot tell apart what is real from what is not.
