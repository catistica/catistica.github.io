Laboratorio del Ruido

Thesis. In collaboration with LUCÍA BELÉN ROVASIO AGUIRRE.

Based on our investigation about noise pollution and urban-stress in the city of Buenos Aires, Laboratorio del ruido is a system of urban installations that resignify urban noise.

On a first instance, the noise of the city is recorded by the tótems spread around the city. The noise is afterwards used in a inmersive-interactive room where the user transmutes the city noise into a calm soundscape.
