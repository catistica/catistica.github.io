Miti Miti

In collaboration with IAN COSANO CATTANEO, VALENTINO DATO and LUCÍA BELÉN ROVASIO AGUIRRE.

Miti Miti is a project of 3D Mapping. It explains through an interactive room the Quinta Monroy project by ELEMENTAL studio, an urban project that was designed for this chilean neighborhood that offers an innovative way of thinking about the problem of social housing.
