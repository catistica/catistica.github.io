Dynasym

For this university project, we had to redesign an assigned object with the aesthetic of a certain designer.

In my case, this pop coffee maker takes its aesthetic from designer Karim Rashid. The concepts revolves around the idea of dynamic asymmetry, where the main characters are the unique curves and vibrant colors.
