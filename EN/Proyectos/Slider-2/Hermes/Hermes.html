ILLUSTRATION AND GRAPHIC

Hermés Competition

In collaboration with LUCÍA BELÉN ROVASIO AGUIRRE.

In this Hermès competiton we had to design the four shop-windows of the Buenos Aires Hermès store. Mantaining the brands essence, we had to reprecent the concept of lightness by designing 2 main shop-windows and 2 secondary ones.

Our concept revolved around the idea of the dream-realm and the surreal. Our shop-windows show a world where dreams are created in bubbles, and where the little dream-monster pops these dreams so they can run free in our reality.
