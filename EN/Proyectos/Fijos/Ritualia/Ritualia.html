RITUALIA

In collaboration with MARA JIMENEZ and VALENTINO DATO.

Ritualia is a university practice of a branding project for based on a selection of objects of the Museo Moderno of Buenos Aires.

Based on a concept revolving dark-magic and mysticism, we designed Ritualias identity, from the logotype and key visuals to the museum display and spatial arrangement. The selected objects helped with the gothic and dark-magic aesthetic.
