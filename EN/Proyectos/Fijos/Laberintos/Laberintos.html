VIDEO AND ANIMATION

LABERINTOS

Labrintos is a university practice for 2D Mapping. With a given soundtrack, we had to create an animation that matched an isometric grid.

Whit this in mind, i explored references with impossible geometries, like the mobile game Monument Valley, and created this surreal, laberinth-like scenery, where the laws of physic don’t apply.
