EN UN VIAJE

This illustration was made for Planteorama, an illustration section of the digital media El Planteo.

With some anime and cartoon references, the idea was to represent cannabis culture through a fantasy approach, playing also with the expression of “estar en un viaje” (a spanish expression for being high).
